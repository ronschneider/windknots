## About Windknots

Windknots is your daily catch of fishing news, tips, and stories from around the web. We automatically aggregate content from trusted fishing publications and present it in one convenient location.

### How It Works

Every day, our automated system:

1. **Fetches** the latest fishing content from top publications and communities
2. **Summarizes** articles using AI to give you the key points quickly
3. **Tags** content automatically so you can filter by topic
4. **Publishes** everything to this site for easy browsing

### Our Sources

We aggregate content from respected fishing publications including:

- Field & Stream
- Outdoor Life
- Sport Fishing Magazine
- Fly Fisherman
- In-Fisherman
- Salt Water Sportsman
- Bassmaster
- And more...

### Topics We Cover

- **Freshwater Fishing** - Bass, trout, walleye, and more
- **Saltwater Fishing** - Offshore, inshore, and surf fishing
- **Gear Reviews** - Rods, reels, tackle, and equipment
- **Fishing News** - Tournaments, records, and regulations
- **Travel** - Fishing destinations and trip reports
- **Fly Tying** - Tips, patterns, and demos
- **Conservation** - Habitat protection and sustainable fishing

### Disclaimer

Windknots is a content aggregator. All articles link to their original sources, and full credit remains with the original publishers. We summarize content to help you discover great fishing articles, but we encourage you to read the full articles at their original sources.
